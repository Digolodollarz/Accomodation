Accommodation Information

We have a database of property owners who are willing to accommodate students in their premises. Why are you waiting, go over and look at the houses on offer . Find your house, contact the owner and that is all there is to it.

We only provide you with the information about the houses. The contract terms you reach with your property owners are up to you, and we won't interfere with the process.
